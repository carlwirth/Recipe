How to cook a Carl Fried Egg

This is my version of a sunny side up egg. However, you do not get that thick yoke that some folks don't like.



    Needed Items
  • One egg
  • Small non-stick pan
  • Stove

    Directions
  1. Pre-heat pan and melt some quality butter.
  2. Rinse off your egg.
  3. Wiggle pan to make sure butter is coating entire bottom and the sides.
  4. Crack the egg and gently break into the pan.
  5. Here is Carl's secret.
  6. Grab a fork
  7. Gently slide the fork tines down into the Albumin (thick part of egg white).
  8. Turn the fork so that you are gently stabbing into the yoke about half way from the bottom.
  9. Gently wiggle the fork so that the yoke can bleed out into the albumin.
  10. Now, gently reverse out of the yoke and then the albumin.
  11. NOW, the yoke will bleed into the albumin.
  12. However, it should stay inside the albumin and NOT spill out over the pan.!!
